Our Vision: Direct from Farm to Family

Root Direct was founded on the principle of transparency and fair trade. We cut out unnecessary middlemen to ensure **farmers across Karnataka receive maximum value** for their high-quality produce and **customers receive fresh, authentic staples** at a reasonable price. Our goal is to build a sustainable and ethical supply chain, strengthening our local communities.

Core Values & Goals

  • **Authenticity:** Providing only the purest, locally sourced staples from registered Karnataka farms.
  • **Fairness:** Ensuring fair and timely payments to our farmer partners.
  • **Sustainability:** Promoting eco-friendly farming and supply practices.
  • **Donations & CSR:** Allocating a portion of our profits to support rural education and sustainable agriculture workshops in Karnataka.
